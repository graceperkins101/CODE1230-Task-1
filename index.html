Superkilen is a public park in an urban area. It is made up of three sections -the red square, the black market and the green park) that are all connected to each other in one long strip that is 1km long.

Image source: http://www.akdn.org/architecture/project/superkilen

The Red Square: consists of bright red, orange and pink concrete floor. Used mainly for sport as the striphas basketball rings and is not accessible by car.
